Privacy Policy — Weighted

Last updated: 19 June 2026

Weighted (“the app”) is a strength-training logger for streetlifting. This policy explains what data the app handles. It is intentionally short because the app collects very little.

What we collect

Nothing leaves your device. All of your training data — workouts, sets, exercises, bodyweight entries, personal records, templates, profile details (such as your name, weight, height and profile photo) and app settings — is stored locally on your iPhone using on-device storage (SQLite). We do not run servers that receive this data, and we do not transmit it to us or to any third party.

Photos

If you choose to add a profile photo or an exercise image, the app accesses your photo library only to let you pick that image. The selected image is stored locally on your device. The app does not upload your photos anywhere.

Subscriptions

Weighted offers an auto-renewable subscription. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. Apple handles billing under Apple’s Privacy Policy. The app receives only whether your subscription is active, in order to unlock features.

Analytics & tracking

The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Data deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you control it completely. You can clear your training history from Profile → Settings → Clear training history, and deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.

Children

Weighted is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the new version at this URL.
