Privacy Policy — Weighted

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Weighted (“the app”) is a strength-training logger for streetlifting. This policy explains what data the app handles. It is intentionally short because the app collects very little.

Your training data

Your workouts stay on your device. All of your training data — workouts, sets, exercises, bodyweight entries, personal records, templates, profile details (such as your weight, height and profile photo) and app settings — is stored locally on your iPhone using on-device storage (SQLite). We do not upload your training data to any server.

Your account

Weighted requires an account so you can sign in and keep your access across reinstalls. When you sign up we collect your email address and a username; if you use Sign in with Apple, we receive the email or private relay address Apple provides. This account information is stored by Supabase, our authentication provider, on its servers, and is used only to sign you in and identify your account — not for marketing, advertising or tracking. Supabase processes it under Supabase’s Privacy Policy. You can request deletion of your account by emailing us (see Contact below).

Photos

If you choose to add a profile photo or an exercise image, the app accesses your photo library only to let you pick that image. The selected image is stored locally on your device. The app does not upload your photos anywhere.

Subscriptions

Weighted offers an auto-renewable subscription. Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store — we never see or store your payment details. To manage your subscription and restore Pro across reinstalls, the app uses RevenueCat, a subscription-management service. RevenueCat receives an app user ID and your purchase and transaction information for this app; it does not receive your training data. RevenueCat processes this under RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy, and Apple handles billing under Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Analytics & tracking

The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Data deletion

Your training data lives only on your device, so you control it completely. You can clear your training history from Profile → Settings → Clear training history, and deleting the app removes all of its on-device data. To delete your account and the email/username held by our authentication provider, email us (see Contact below) and we will remove it.

Children

Weighted is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the new version at this URL.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@weightedapp.com.
